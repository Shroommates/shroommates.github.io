Shroommates

About

Shroommates is a third-person platformer where players play as a sentient mushroom trying to grow in secret in a UCSC student's apartment. The mushroom can grow by consuming food at staying out of direct sunlight. Players must adapt to different sizes, as size dynamically alters speed, hiding ability, and places you can get to. Complete objectives while trying to keep out of sight off the AI human!
